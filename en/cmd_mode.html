3. List of queries

3.1. Global operations

Query

Action

:n

Create a new database.

:o

Open an existing database.

:q

Close blunderDB.

3.2. Interact with a position

Query

Action

:i

Import a position via a text file (txt).

:w

Save the current position in the current library.

:w!

After editing an existing position, modify it in the database.

:w toto titi …

Save the current position in the libraries toto, titi, …

:w -toto

Remove the current position from the toto library.

:LS

List the libraries to which the current position belongs.

:D

Delete the current position.

3.3. Manage the libraries

Query

Action

:e toto

Open the toto library.

:mv titi

Rename the current library to titi.

:mv toto titi

Rename the toto library to titi.

:cp titi

Copy the current library to the titi library.

:cp toto titi

Copy the toto library to titi.

:d

Delete the current library.

:d toto

Delete the toto library.

:ls

List the libraries.

3.4. Search for positions

Warning

In the position search, by default, blunderDB takes into account the current pawn structure, ignoring the position of the board and the score. To consider the board position or the score, it must be explicitly mentioned in the query.

Note

blunderDB considers that a backward pawn (backchecker) is a pawn located between point 24 and point 14.

Note

blunderDB considers that the number of pawns in the zone is the number of pawns located between point 12 and point 1.

Tip

The parameters for filtering positions can be combined arbitrarily.

Query

Action

:s cube

Filter the positions that match the current cube configuration.

:s score

Filter the positions that match the current score.

:s o7

Filter the positions with at least 7 checkers off.

:s p-20,30

Filter the positions where player 1 has between 20 pips ahead and 30 pips behind in the race.

:s p-20,30

Filter the positions where player 1 has at least 10 pips ahead.

:s p>110

Filter the positions where player 1 has at least 110 pips behind.

:s P5,40

Filter the positions with a race difference between 5 and 40 pips.

:s k5

Filter the positions where player 1 has 5 backcheckers.

:s K2

Filter the positions where player 2 has 2 backcheckers.

:s z8

Filter the positions where player 1 has 8 checkers in the zone.

:s Z6

Filter the positions where player 2 has 6 checkers in the zone.

:s e800,1200

Filter the positions where player 1 has an equity between 800 and 1200.

:s e<200

Filter the positions where player 1 has an equity of less than 200.

:s e>400

Filter the positions where player 1 has an equity greater than 400.

:s w40,60

Filter the positions where player 1 has winning chances between 40% and 60%.

:s w<25

Filter the positions where player 1 has winning chances of less than 25%.

:s w>68

Filtre les positions où le joueur 1 a des chances de gain supérieures à 68%.

:s g14,22

Filter the positions where player 1 has gammon chances between 14% and 22%.

:s g<27

Filter the positions where player 1 has gammon chances of less than 27%.

:s g>45

Filter the positions where player 1 has gammon chances greater than 45%.

:s bg4,8

Filter the positions where player 1 has backgammon chances between 4% and 8%.

:s bg<10

Filter the positions where player 1 has backgammon chances of less than 10%.

:s bg>5

Filter the positions where player 1 has backgammon chances greater than 5%.

:s W40,60

Filter the positions where player 2 has winning chances between 40% and 60%.

:s W<25

Filter the positions where player 2 has winning chances of less than 25%.

:s W>68

Filter the positions where player 2 has winning chances greater than 68%.

:s G14,22

Filter the positions where player 2 has gammon chances between 14% and 22%.

:s G<27

Filter the positions where player 2 has gammon chances of less than 27%.

:s G>45

Filter the positions where player 2 has gammon chances greater than 45%.

:s BG4,8

Filter the positions where player 2 has backgammon chances between 4% and 8%.

:s BG<10

Filter the positions where player 2 has backgammon chances of less than 10%.

:s BG>5

Filter the positions where player 2 has backgammon chances greater than 5%.
