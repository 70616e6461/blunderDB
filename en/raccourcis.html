4.3. Position editing via keyboard

In EDIT mode, letters are displayed for each point and the cube. To add or remove one or more checkers on a point, type the corresponding letter of the point followed by the number of checkers to add. If the letter is entered in lowercase, the checkers belong to the bottom player. If the letter is entered in uppercase, the edited checkers belong to the top player. To remove checkers, precede the number with a minus sign ‘-’. Adding a prime or a sequence of spares is done by indicating the letter of the first point, a dash ‘-’, and then the letter of the last point. To change the score, enter a number corresponding to the away score of the bottom player, a comma ‘,’, and then enter a number corresponding to the away score of the top player. To cancel an entry, press ‘ESC’.
